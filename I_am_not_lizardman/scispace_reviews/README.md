# SciSpace Review Artifacts

Last reviewed: 2026-03-04

This folder contains screenshots generated by the SciSpace AI reading assistant.

They are preserved as **AI-generated review artifacts** that summarize how an automated tool interpreted the papers at the time they were uploaded.

## Important clarification

These images are **not peer review**, **not academic endorsement**, and **not validation of the research claims**.

They simply record the output of an automated literature reading tool.

Readers interested in evaluating the work should read the original PDFs directly.

## Why these files are kept

The screenshots are preserved for transparency and historical context, showing how an AI system summarized the papers when they were first uploaded.

They should not be interpreted as academic evaluation.
