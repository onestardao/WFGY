## FAQ

### Is this mysticism?

No.

This lab does not ask you to believe a mythic or symbolic claim.

It asks whether strange material can be lifted into a structured research kernel.

### Why use strange themes?

Because strange themes stress-test the engine.

If an engine can turn difficult symbolic material into definitions, functions, lemmas, tests, and counterexamples, it is not only a normal Q&A tool.

### Does every strange output become true?

No.

The goal is not instant truth.

The goal is structure: a kernel that can be inspected, attacked, tested, and improved.

### What is the fastest way to try it?

Copy this:

```text
shoot + Death + AI
```

Or:

```text
shoot + Meme + Epidemic
```

### What should I do after it runs?

Ask AI to attack the output.

Look for weak definitions, fake structure, missing counterexamples, and the next repair command.
